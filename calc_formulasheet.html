Formula Sheet

General Formulae
\[ f (x) \] \[ f'(x) \]
\[ c \] \[ 0 \]
\[ c \cdot u \] \[ c \cdot \frac{d}{dx} u \]
\[ x^n \] \[ n \cdot x^{n-1} \]
\[ u \pm v \] \[ \frac{d}{dx} u \pm \frac{d}{dx} v \]
\[ u \cdot v \] \[ u \cdot \frac{d}{dx} v + v \cdot \frac{d}{dx} u \]
\[ \frac{hi}{lo} \] \[ \frac{lo \cdot d hi - hi \cdot d lo}{lo^2} \]
\[ f (g (x)) \] \[ f'(g(x)) \cdot g'(x) \]

Trigonometric Formulae
\[ \sin (x) \] \[ \cos (x) \]
\[ \cos (x) \] \[ - \sin (x) \]
\[ \tan (x) \] \[ \sec^2 (x) \]
\[ \csc (x) \] \[ - \csc (x) \cdot \cot (x) \]
\[ \sec (x) \] \[ \sec (x) \cdot \tan (x) \]
\[ \cot (x) \] \[ - \csc^2 (x) \]
\[ \sin^{-1}(x) \] \[ \frac{1}{\sqrt{1 - x^2}} \]
\[ \cos^{-1}(x) \] \[ - \frac{1}{\sqrt{1 - x^2}} \]
\[ \tan^{-1}(x) \] \[ \frac{1}{1 + x^2} \]
\[ \cot^{-1}(x) \] \[ - \frac{1}{1 + x^2} \]
\[ \sec^{-1}(x) \] \[ \frac{1}{|x| \cdot \sqrt{x^2 - 1}} \]
\[ \csc^{-1}(x) \] \[ - \frac{1}{|x| \cdot \sqrt{x^2 - 1}} \]

Hyperbolic Formulae
\[ \sinh (x) \] \[ \cosh (x) \]
\[ \cosh (x) \] \[ \sinh (x) \]
\[ \tanh (x) \] \[ \operatorname{sech}^2 (x) \]
\[ \coth (x) \] \[ - \operatorname{csch}^2 (x) \]
\[ \operatorname{sech} (x) \] \[- \operatorname{sech} (x) \cdot \tanh (x) \]
\[ \operatorname{csch} (x) \] \[ - \operatorname{csch} (x) \cdot \coth (x) \]
\[ \sinh^{-1}(x) \] \[ \frac{1}{\sqrt{x^2 + 1}} \]
\[ \cosh^{-1}(x) \] \[ \frac{1}{\sqrt{x^2 - 1}} \]
\[ \tanh^{-1}(x) \] \[ \frac{1}{1 - x^2} \]
\[ \coth^{-1}(x) \] \[ \frac{1}{1 - x^2} \]
\[ \operatorname{sech}^{-1}(x) \] \[ - \frac{1}{|x| \cdot \sqrt{1 - x^2}} \]
\[ \operatorname{csch}^{-1}(x) \] \[ - \frac{1}{|x| \cdot \sqrt{1 + x^2}} \]

Logarithmic and Exponential Formulae
\[ e^x \] \[ e^x \]
\[ a^x \] \[ a^x \cdot \ln (x) \]
\[ x^x \] \[ x^x \cdot (\ln (x) + 1) \]
\[ \ln (x) \] \[ \frac{1}{x} \]
\[ \log _a (x) \] \[ \frac{1}{x \cdot \ln (x)} \]
