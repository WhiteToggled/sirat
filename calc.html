Derivatives

Calculating velocity and changes in velocity are important uses of calculus, but it is far more widespread than that. Calculus is important in all branches of mathematics, science, and engineering, and it is critical to analysis in business and health as well. In this chapter, we explore one of the main tools of calculus, the derivative, and show convenient ways to calculate derivatives. We apply these rules to a variety of functions in this chapter so that we can then explore applications of these techniques.


What to expect?

Whether you're just beginning with calculus or looking to strengthen your understanding of derivatives, this resource provides a comprehensive look at one of calculus's most essential concepts. Here, you'll find a structured guide to mastering derivatives, from foundational definitions to advanced applications. Each chapter builds upon the last, breaking down complex ideas into manageable lessons.

Expect detailed explanations, step-by-step examples, and practical applications in physics, engineering, and beyond. By the end, you'll have the tools to tackle real-world problems and understand how derivatives help describe the dynamic changes that shape the world around us.
